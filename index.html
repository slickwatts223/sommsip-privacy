SommSip Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026
Short version: SommSip does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Who We Are

SommSip is a wine tasting journal app developed by Benjamin Williams. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data We Do Not Collect

SommSip does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing or search history
  • Financial information

Data You Create in the App

When you use SommSip, you create tasting notes, ratings, wine entries, and optionally attach label photos. All of this data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only — it never leaves your iPhone
  • Not transmitted to any server — SommSip makes no network requests of any kind
  • Not accessible to us — we have no back-end and no way to view your data
  • Deleted when you delete the app — uninstalling SommSip removes all associated data

Photos

SommSip can optionally access your photo library to let you attach a wine label photo to a tasting entry. Photos are:

  • Only accessed when you explicitly choose to add a photo
  • Stored on your device within the app's local database
  • Never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your iPhone may back up app data (including your tasting notes) to your personal iCloud account as part of Apple's standard device backup. This is controlled entirely by you in your iPhone Settings. SommSip does not initiate or control this backup. Your data in iCloud is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

SommSip uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers. There are no third parties involved in the operation of this app.

Children's Privacy

SommSip is an app about wine and alcohol. It is intended for users who are 21 years of age or older in the United States, or of legal drinking age in their country. The app is not directed at children under 17 and we do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 17.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: support@sommsip.app
